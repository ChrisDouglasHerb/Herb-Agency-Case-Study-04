CMAAS · THE SETUP
02

Where we started, and what we changed.

The brand had the product, the audience, and the spend. What it did not have was a stable place to put media. We rebuilt the setup from the ground up.

THE PROBLEM

A funnel built on workarounds.

  • Repeated account shutdowns, reset learning data with every relaunch
  • Vague creative, no direct cannabis verbiage or imagery allowed
  • Cloaked landing pages, multi-click consumer journey to checkout
  • Attribution distrust, no reliable ROAS, no real CAC visibility
  • No senior media buyer or strategist owning the work
THE APPROACH

One account, direct funnel, real data.

  • Migrated to Herb's compliance ad account, direct relationship at Meta
  • Direct cannabis verbiage and imagery served native to feed
  • Direct-to-site funnel behind a simple age gate, no cloaking
  • 100% prospecting with customer suppression in place from day one
  • Senior strategist + media buyer paired with full attribution dashboard

CMAAS · WHY IT COMPOUNDS
05

Why this works, and keeps working.

Compliance ad account access is the unlock, but it is the compounding system around it that makes the curve bend the right way.

01

One account, one learning history.

No shutdowns to recover from, no pixel data starting from scratch. The algorithm gets sharper every week, not reset every month.

02

Direct-to-site, no funnel leakage.

A simple age gate replaces cloaked pages and 6-click journeys. Less drop-off, cleaner intent, stronger landing page rates.

03

100% prospecting with customer suppression.

Existing customers excluded from prospecting audiences. Every dollar drives net-new acquisition. Remarketing handled separately by programmatic display.

04

Real attribution, not last-click guesswork.

7-day click / 1-day view, mapped to purchase events on the site. ROAS, CAC, and LTV visibility you can take to a board meeting.
